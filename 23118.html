系统需求

    • 操作系统: Win 10 (64 bit) or later (Win 11 recommended for Intel 12th and 13th gen CPUs)
    • 处理器: AMD Ryzen 5 2600X Intel Core i7-8700K
    • 内存: 16(Dual-channel) GB RAM
    • 显卡: AMD Radeon RX 580 (8GB) NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1070 (8GB)
    • DirectX 版本: 12
    • 网络: 宽带互联网连接
