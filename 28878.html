关于此游戏
Zumbi Blocks 2


Zumbi Blocks 2 is a zombie survival game. It’s going to be available for free while it’s in early development.

Much will change in terms of gameplay, as it will move towards a base building / wave survival type of game.

Kill: fight zombies with melee and ranged weapons, both in first person or third person mode.

Loot: explore the procedurally generated city, to find resources.

Overcome: defeat bosses that live in interest points along the island.

Upgrade: unlock loadout tiers, that you can bring to the match with you.

系统需求
最低配置:
操作系统: Windows 10 or 11. (32 or 64 bit)
处理器: Intel Core i3-7300 / AMD Ryzen 3 1300X
内存: 1 GB RAM
显卡: Nvidia GT 1030 / AMD Vega 11
DirectX 版本: 10
存储空间: 需要 300 MB 可用空间
声卡: Yes
支持 VR: No
附注事项: Believed to run game on 720p, lowest settings, at 60 FPS, most of the time.
